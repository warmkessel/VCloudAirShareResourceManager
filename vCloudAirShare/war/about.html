About vCloud Air Share


What is vCloud Air Share

vCloud Air Share is a new service that allows anyone with a Twitter ID to easily access a Virtual Machine in VMware's vCloud Air environment. To gain access, users simply check out a Virtual Machine and SSH in. It's that simple to use a VM with vCloud Air Share.

vCloud Air Share was created with the aim of exposing developers and IT professionals to Cloud Computing. In most cases, Cloud Computing solutions are implemented on a large scale for company use. We aim to provide individuals personal access to cloud computing technology so they can explore its features.

Air Share is intended for IT practitioners, developers, and anyone else interested in learning DevOps tools, cloud services, Photon, Lightwave and other VMware Developer targeted technologies. Users can explore the VM's features, learn VMware targeted technologies, or use it for a personal project.


Why You Should Try vCloud Air Share

No Installation required

Simply SSH to the Virtual Machine for instant access.
A Multitude of Operating Systems

Learn the specific features of different operating systems quickly and conveniently.
Get to know vCloud Air

Familiarize yourself with the vCloud Air Environment.
Use anywhere

You don't have to drag your computer everywhere to use vCloud Air Share, it's on the Cloud!
